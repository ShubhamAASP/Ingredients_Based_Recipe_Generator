Technical Details

Our recipe generator uses a GPT2 model fine-tuned on a dataset of over 100,000 recipes. The model was Fine-tuned by the our team and has been optimized for generating coherent, detailed recipes from simple ingredient lists.

Key technical features:

  • Based on the GPT2 architecture
  • Fine-tuned specifically for recipe generation
  • Special tokens for recipe structure (ingredients, directions, etc.)
  • Beam search and temperature sampling for creative variations
